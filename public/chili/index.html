Hello Chili

Simple chili recipe

Ingredients

2 pounds lean ground beef

2 packages McCormick Chili Seasoning Mix

2 cans (14 1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained

2 cans (15 to 16 ounces) kidney beans, undrained

Instructions

1. Brown meat in large skillet on medium-high heat. Drain fat.

2. Stir in Seasoning Mix, tomatoes and beans. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Serve with assorted toppings, if desired.
